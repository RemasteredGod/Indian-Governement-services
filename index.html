Entry Pathways Guide

Step-by-step guide to enter government services

UPSC Civil Services

1

Check Eligibility

Age: 21-32 years, Graduation from recognized university

2

Apply Online

Register on UPSC portal, pay fee, submit application

3

Prepare & Appear

Prelims → Mains → Interview (1-2 years preparation)

4

Training

Foundation Course + Service-specific training (2 years)

Defense Services (NDA)

1

Check Eligibility

Age: 16.5-19.5 years, 12th with PCM (unmarried)

2

NDA Exam

Written exam twice yearly, Mathematics & General Ability

3

SSB Interview

5-day selection process including psychological tests

4

Training

3 years at NDA + 1 year at respective service academy

Technical Services

1

Check Eligibility

Age: 21-30 years, B.Tech from recognized university

2

UPSC ESE

Engineering Services Exam - Prelims + Mains + Interview

3

Service Allocation

Based on rank and preference (Railways, CPWD, etc.)

4

Training

Service-specific training (6 months - 1 year)

How This Calculator Works

Our algorithm analyzes your profile against eligibility criteria for various government services and ranks them by match percentage.

  • Age and qualification determine basic eligibility
  • Preferences affect match scoring
  • Results show top 5 matching services

Key Considerations

When choosing a government service career:

  • Exam difficulty and competition ratio
  • Long-term career growth opportunities
  • Location postings and transfers
  • Work culture and responsibilities
