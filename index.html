Entry Pathways Guide

Step-by-step guide to enter government services

UPSC Civil Services

1

Check Eligibility

Age: 21-32 years, Graduation from recognized university

2

Apply Online

Register on UPSC portal, pay fee, submit application

3

Prepare & Appear

Prelims → Mains → Interview (1-2 years preparation)

4

Training

Foundation Course + Service-specific training (2 years)

Defense Services (NDA)

1

Check Eligibility

Age: 16.5-19.5 years, 12th with PCM (unmarried)

2

NDA Exam

Written exam twice yearly, Mathematics & General Ability

3

SSB Interview

5-day selection process including psychological tests

4

Training

3 years at NDA + 1 year at respective service academy

Technical Services

1

Check Eligibility

2

UPSC ESE

Engineering Services Exam - Prelims + Mains + Interview

3

Service Allocation

Based on rank and preference (Railways, CPWD, etc.)

4

Training

Service-specific training (6 months - 1 year)
